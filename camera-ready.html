DTwin 2026

ACM MobiCom Workshop on Digital Twins over NextG Wireless Networks


Austin, TX, USA
October 30, 2026

Co-located with ACM MobiCom 2026

SUBMISSION AND REGISTRATION

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • The workshop accepts only original and previously unpublished papers.
  • All submissions must be double-blind and formatted in standard ACM camera-ready format (double-column, 10pt font).
  • Technical papers: up to 6 pages including references (with the option of 2 extra pages at a cost per extra page).
  • Demo papers: up to 2 pages including references.
  • Camera-ready papers should use the ACM template: http://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template. In LaTeX, use \documentclass[sigconf, 10pt]{acmart}.
  • Submission portal: to be announced.

IMPORTANT DATES

  • Paper Submission: June 5, 2026
  • Notification of Acceptance: July 31, 2026
  • Camera-ready: August 21, 2026
  • Workshop Date: October 30, 2026 (Full Day)

REGISTRATION NOTES

  • No remote participation.
  • Attendees must hold a full registration + workshop, or a workshop-only registration.
