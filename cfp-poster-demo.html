CALL FOR POSTERS AND DEMOS

We especially encourage poster and demo submissions showcasing DT experimental platforms, prototypes, and real-world applications over NextG wireless networks. The poster/demo session is a dynamic forum for sharing early-stage results, getting feedback, and fostering technical discussion among academic, industry, and government participants.

Suitable submissions include, but are not limited to, demonstrations of digital twin platforms, prototype implementations, measurement studies, and real-world deployments related to the topics listed in the Call for Papers.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Demo papers must not exceed 2 pages in ACM double-column format, 10pt font, including references.
  • All demo titles should start with "Demo:".
  • Submissions must include author names and affiliations.
  • Submission portal details will be announced soon.

A Best Poster/Demo Award will be presented to recognize outstanding contributions.
